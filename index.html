Witney Plant & Tool Hire Oxfordshire

The UK's Plant & Tool Hiring Specialists

Witney Plant Hire Ltd is a specialist Plant and tool Hire Oxfordshire, a Company based in the Market town of Witney in the Oxfordshire Cotswolds. We are a family run business founded in March 2000, focused on delivering a quality service at a competitive price. Since formation the business has not only developed into a successful and well respected plant equipment hire company, but we have also expanded and are now offering a comprehensive range of support services that enables us to deliver exceptional diversity and value.

We specialise in self-drive Heavy and light plant hire site equipment, Tool Hire Oxfordshire, Portable Site Accommodation and general construction equipment Hire. We run a modern large fleet of plant and tool equipment from Oxfordshire which is wholly maintained by us. We have recently added to our Hire Fleet a Keestrack Argo TK141 Dual Crusher and a Keestrack K3 Scalping Combo Screener.

We continually invest in the latest haulage equipment, and own an extensive fleet of modern vehicles to cater for all the diverse haulage demands of the Plant Hire business. Our versatile fleet of vehicles as well as supporting our Plant Hire Customers are also available for most light and heavy haulage requirements.

To further support our Customers we have two Scania Crane Lorries, including a 32 Tonne Meter and 85 Tonne Meter. These HIAB lorries are managed by trained specialist drivers and are suitable for general lifting, for even the most compact of site locations as well as suitable for heavy and specialist lifts.

We also operate a fleet of modern 8-wheeled Tipper Lorries and provide a comprehensive Tipper Service. Our highly trained drivers are EPIC trained and experienced in construction waste removal as well as the haulage of general aggregates.

Witney Plant Hire Ltd are members of the CPA (Construction Plant-hire Association) and all Plant is hired under CPA Terms and Conditions.
