Family-Run Hire Specialists Serving Oxfordshire & the Cotswolds

Witney Plant Hire Ltd is a specialist plant and tool hire company in Oxfordshire, based in the market town of Witney in the Cotswolds. We are a family-run business, founded in March 2000, focused on delivering a quality service at a competitive price. Since then we have grown into a successful, well-respected plant hire company and expanded into a comprehensive range of support services that bring exceptional diversity and value to our customers.

We specialise in self-drive heavy and light plant, tool hire, portable site accommodation and general construction equipment hire across Oxfordshire. Our large, modern fleet is wholly maintained in-house, and recent additions include a Keestrack Argo TK141 dual crusher and a Keestrack K3 scalping combo screener.

We continually invest in the latest haulage equipment and run an extensive fleet of modern vehicles to meet the diverse demands of the plant hire business. As well as supporting our plant hire customers, this versatile fleet is available for most light and heavy haulage requirements.

To further support our customers we operate two Scania crane lorries, with 32 tonne-metre and 85 tonne-metre HIAB capacities. Managed by trained specialist drivers, they handle general lifting in even the most compact site locations, as well as heavy and specialist lifts.

We also operate a fleet of modern 8-wheel tipper lorries and provide a comprehensive tipper service. Our highly trained, EPIC-accredited drivers are experienced in construction waste removal and the haulage of general aggregates.

Witney Plant Hire Ltd is a member of the CPA (Construction Plant-hire Association), and all plant is hired under CPA terms and conditions.
