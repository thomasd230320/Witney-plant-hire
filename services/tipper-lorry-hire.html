Tipper Lorry Hire Oxfordshire

8-wheel tipper hire, muck-away and aggregates across Oxfordshire.

We run a fleet of modern 8-wheeled tipper lorries fitted with rollover sheeting, onboard weighing and reversing cameras. Available for muck-away and construction waste removal, and for the supply and transport of aggregates including ballast, sands, gravels, shingle, Type 1, crushed limestone and crushed concrete.

For a quote, machine availability or to confirm delivery to your postcode, please call our hire desk on 01993 708020 or email enquiries@witneyplanthire.com.
