All equipment is hired under the Construction Plant-hire Association (CPA) Model Conditions for the Hiring of Plant. By signing below the Hirer agrees that:

1. The Hirer is responsible for the equipment from delivery until collection, including against loss, theft and damage.
2. The equipment must be used only by competent, trained operators and for its intended purpose.
3. Hire charges run for the agreed period; the minimum hire period is one day.
4. The Hirer will allow safe access for delivery, inspection and collection.
5. Quoted figures are estimates excluding VAT, delivery, fuel and insurance, and are confirmed by Witney Plant Hire Ltd before hire commences.

This online agreement records intent to hire and is confirmed by our hire desk before any equipment leaves our yard.
